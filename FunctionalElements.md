### From Ucsc
see https://genome.ucsc.edu

  https://genome.ucsc.edu/cgi-bin/hgTables  

### From Ensembl
see https://www.ensembl.org

https://www.ensembl.org/info/data/ftp/index.html

### From ENCODE
